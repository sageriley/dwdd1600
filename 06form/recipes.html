BREAKFAST

Banana Muffins

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 overripe banana
  • 6 Tbs salted butter
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract click here for substitution
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
Directions:
  1. Heat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Spray or lightly butter muffin tin.
  3. In a large bowl, whip together the sugar and one fo the bananas until well blended.
  4. Add the cooled melted butter, the egg, and the vanilla and beat well.
  5. Mix in the flour, baking soda, and salt until just incorporated.
  6. Peel and mash the remaining banana witha fork on a small plate. Fold into the batter.
  7. Scoop the batter into the muffin tins and fill them 3/4 of the way.
  8. Bake until a toothpick stuck in the muffins comes out clean, 20-25 minutes.
